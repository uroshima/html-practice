Ingredients

  • white beans
  • onion
  • tomato
  • sausage
  • fresh mint

Grafce tafce

Description

This is traditional macedonian dish eaten on Fridays

Directions

  1. Put the beans in water and cook it on medium heat.
  2. After half an hour add one diced yellow onion and one diced tomato.
  3. In a pan and high temperature sear the sausages for about 6 minutes
  4. Ten minutes before the beans is ready, add the sausages and the mint in the beans pot.
  5. After 10 minutes, place the whole thing in a ceramic pan and place it in the oven for about 20 minutes on 375 degrees.
  6. Then take out the pan and enjoy Tavce Gravce.
